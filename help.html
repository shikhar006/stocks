Lookup Prices

To look up stock prices:

  • On the start menu click the "Lookup" button
  • Then enter the stock symbol
Examples of stock symbols:
Company Symbol
Apple AAPL
Amazon AMZN
Tesla TSLA
Facebook FB
Netflix NFLX

Buy stock

To buy stock:

  • Go to the main menu
  • Click buy stock
  • Enter the stock symbol
  • Enter the Quantity
  • Click Enter (This is just a game, you won't actually buy stock)
  • Click Continue

Sell stock

  • Go to the main menu
  • Click sell stock
  • Enter the stock symbol
  • Click enter
  • Enter the quantity
  • Click continue (This is just a game, you won't actually buy stock)

View stock

  • Go to the main menu
  • Click on view
